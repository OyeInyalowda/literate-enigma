Original Racquetball Specifications

  • The player controls a tall and thin rectangle on the right side of the screen. They may move it up and down with the arrow keys, but not left and right.
  • A ball will be launched from the left side of the screen towards the right, at a diagonal.
  • The player will try to bounce the ball back towards the left side of the screen. Every time they do, they get a point.
  • If the player misses the ball and it goes beyond the right side of the screen, the player loses a point. The ball will then be launched again from the left side of the screen.
  • The ball should bounce off of the top and bottom and left side of the screen.
  • Display the score at the top left of the screen using the text() function.
